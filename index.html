About Us

mrpepe is a variant of pepe. Pepe the Frog is a popular internet meme that originated from a comic series created by artist Matt Furie. The character gained widespread recognition and became a meme in various online communities, often depicted as a green anthropomorphic frog with various expressions. Over time, Pepe became associated with different emotions and contexts, and unfortunately, it was also adopted as a symbol by certain hate groups, which led to controversy and attempts to reclaim or disassociate Pepe from those negative connotations. It's worth noting that the original intention behind Pepe was not meant to be associated with hate or offensive content, but rather as a lighthearted and relatable character.
